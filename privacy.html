Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how sglabs collects, uses, and protects information when you use our app and connected platform integrations.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Account and authorization data needed for integrations (for example OAuth tokens)
  • Content metadata you provide (titles, captions, prompts)
  • Operational logs needed to process jobs and troubleshoot errors

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Generate and process your videos
  • Upload or publish content only when you request it
  • Maintain system security, reliability, and fraud prevention

4. TikTok and YouTube Data

When you connect TikTok or YouTube, we access only the permissions you grant. We use those permissions solely to upload/publish your content on your behalf. We do not sell your platform data.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers required to run the app (for example cloud, AI, and platform APIs) and only to the extent needed for service delivery.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for operations, legal compliance, and security. You may request deletion of stored integration data where applicable.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure.

8. Your Choices

You can disconnect platform integrations at any time and stop using the app. You may request deletion of stored personal data by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

sglabs is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use after updates indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: privacy@sglabs.app
